What?

Brainhack is a global conference bringing together researchers from multiple disciplines to work together on innovative projects related to neuroscience. Brainhack Warwick will feature presentations from key researchers in our community as well as video links to the global community. Visit BrainHack Global 2018 to learn more about events worldwide.
